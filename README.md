## ✨ About the App
**MyDesck PRO** is a professional accounting and management dashboard designed originally for Tourism Offices, now evolving to support various business sectors like Supermarkets and Retail Shops.

### 🌟 Key Features:
* **Smart Dashboard:** Track profits, losses, and business percentages with interactive charts.
* **Digital Invoicing:** Say goodbye to paper receipt books (لا داعي لدفاتر الإيصالات).
* **Accountant Friendly:** Export all your financial data easily to your accountant.
* **Auto-Updates:** The app stays up-to-date automatically without manual downloads.
* **Multi-Language:** Full support for English, Hebrew, and Arabic (RTL support).
